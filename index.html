About

Dan Light is an LA-based music composer and producer creating works primarily for Film and TV. With a background ranging from Jazz, Pop, Folk, Classical, and Electronic, Dan offers a distinct and modern voice in music for media. His music has appeared on nearly every major network and streaming service, including CBS, ABC, NBC, FOX, TBS, Netflix, Hulu, Apple TV+, Disney+, Epix, and more.

Originally from Connecticut, Dan relocated to Los Angeles after graduating from Wesleyan University.

Dan formerly worked with composer Jim Dooley out of Hans Zimmer's Remote Control Productions on projects such as Netflix's A Series of Unfortunate Events and TNT's The Last Ship. In 2020, Dan contributed music to Leo Birenberg and Christophe Beck's score to Epix's docu-series, Helter Skelter. Currently, Dan works with composer Zach Robinson and has contributed music to his score for Netflix's Wrestlers, Peacock's Twisted Metal, Roku's Weird: The Al Yankovic Story, and more.
